Our Story

PASSION
MEETS
PRECISION

Born in the heart of Thrissur, The Retrospect Studio was founded on a single belief — that every classic deserves a second life, and every luxury car deserves nothing less than perfection.

We are Kerala's premier destination for luxury car restoration and customization. Our craftsmen combine old-world artisanship with modern precision engineering, breathing new life into machines that were built to endure. From full frame-off restorations to bespoke modifications, every project that passes through our doors is treated as a work of art.
